Marinara Sauce

This is a multi purpose, simple sauce. It is as good as the ingredients you use.

Ingredients

  • 1 28oz can peeled, whole tomatoes
  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 2 Garlic cloves, slightly smashed and peeled
  • salt
  • pepper
  • basil, oregano, thyme all optional

Directions

  1. In a saute pan, heat garlic cloves gently until starting to brown. Discard.
  2. Carefully add canned tomatoes, it may splatter.
  3. Mash up tomatoes with potato masher or fork.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add dried or fresh herbs to taste. Leave out if making for future use.
