Linguini with Clam Sauce

This is a white clam sauce. For red, just add a 14oz can of tomatoes!

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 can chopped clams
  • Optional: 1 dozen fresh little neck clams
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 Tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Ground black pepper
  • 8oz linguini

Directions

  1. Start a pot of water to boil. Add generous amount of salt to boil and add pasta.
  2. Add olive oil to a saute pan on medium heat.
  3. Gently saute garlic until starting to brown.
  4. If using fresh clams, add them to the pan and cover until they open. Discard unopened clams. Remove clams.
  5. Add juice from canned clams and simmer very gently.
  6. When the pasta is finished, add the canned clams and parsley to the clam juice.
  7. Add linguini to pan with a bit of pasta water. Simmer for a minute. Add fresh clams
