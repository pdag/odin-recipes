Tortellini Soup

This is a non-creamy, chicken soup with Tortellini, sausage and vegetables

Ingredients

  • 3 Italian sausage, cut up or crumbled
  • 4 Carrots chopped
  • 4 Celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 Tsp olive oil
  • 8 cups Chicken broth
  • 1 Large package Tortellini

Directions

  1. In a large pot, sear sausage over medium with olive oil. Remove to a plate.
  2. Add carrots, onion and celery to the pot and saute for 8 minutes.
  3. Add the broth and sausage to the pot along with any juices and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Add Tortellini and cook until done as directed by the package.
